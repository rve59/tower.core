# TOWER Product Management Plan (000100)

## Executive Summary
TOWER is a next-generation regulatory reporting platform designed to modernize the FERC and XBRL ecosystem. By leveraging high-performance data structures and Agentic AI, TOWER transitions compliance from a periodic burden into a continuous operational advantage.

## Product Families & Roadmap

### 1. TOWER Core (Gen 1): Mechanical Excellence & Reliability
*   **Mission**: Provide a "Zero-Hiccup" filing environment for time-pressured regulatory cycles.
*   **Key Features**: 
    *   **Fail-Safe UI/UX**: Guided navigation for **Young Filers** and **Tribal Utilities** to ensure error-free submissions (Form 1/714).
    *   **Ladybug Engine**: High-speed columnar graph validation.
*   **Target**: Entities without legacy compliance teams; first-time reporters.

### 2. TOWER Core Enhanced (Gen 2): Operational Integration
*   **Mission**: Bridge the gap between raw data and cross-jurisdictional reporting (Data-First).
*   **Key Features**: 
    *   **Utility-Grade iXBRL**: Numbers-first xHTML rendering for international (EU/ESEF) mandates—eliminating "Agency Fluff."
    *   **Multi-Output Bridge**: Unified entry mapping to FERC, ESG (CSRD), and State requirements.
    *   **Structural Fingerprinting (TopoMap)**: Identifying identity and structural drift across reporting periods.

### 3. TOWER Agentic (Gen 3): Cognitive Automation & Grant Compliance
*   **Mission**: Delegate the intellectual burden of audit and "Dual-Filing" to an AI agent.
*   **Key Features**: 
    *   **Agentic Dual-Filing**: Automated drafting of **DOE Progress Reports** (SF-425 + Technical Narratives) directly from FERC XBRL datasets.
    *   **Semantic Reconciliation**: Automating the proof of identity between ERP and Disclosed facts.

## Stakeholder Matrix & Function Allocation

| Stakeholder | Key Functionality | Value Driver |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Young Filers** | Guided Templates, No-code Mapping | Compliance Speed |
| **Auditors** | Pre-Audit Shield, Version Diffing | Risk Mitigation |
| **Regulators** | Large-scale Batch Ingestion, Fraud Detection | Oversight Efficiency |
| **Data Analysts** | High-fidelity Aggregated Feeds | Market Insight |

## Continuous Validation (CV) Strategy
TOWER promotes a "Shift-Left" approach to data quality. By implementing State-Aware Validation, we allow users to monitor data integrity in real-time without requiring final totals to be calculated, thus preventing the "Submission Cliff" at deadline.

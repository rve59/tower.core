# TOWER Product Registry (000101)

This registry tracks the specific product lines and SKU-equivalents within the TOWER ecosystem.

## Family: TOWER Core (Mechanical)

### TOWER-V (Validator)
*   **Description**: Command-line and API-based XBRL validation engine.
*   **Target**: DevOps teams, Internal IT.
*   **Generation**: Gen 1.

### TOWER-E (Enhanced Reliability)
*   **Description**: Web-based "Zero-Hiccup" filing environment. Includes **Guided Templates** for first-time filers.
*   **Target**: **Tribal Utility Authorities (TUAs)**, Young Filers, and Small Utilities.
*   **Generation**: Gen 1.

## Family: TOWER Agentic (Cognitive)

### TOWER-G (Grant Compliance)
*   **Description**: Agentic bridge for "Dual-Filing" (FERC ↔ DOE Grant Reports). Auto-SF-425 generation.
*   **Target**: Grant-funded TUAs, Municipalities.
*   **Generation**: Gen 3.

### TOWER-S (Expert Library)
*   **Description**: A library of specialized in-house built audit skills (e.g., IFRS-16 Module, FERC-Form-1 Auditor).
*   **Detailed Catalog**: [Expert System Catalog (000118)](file:///home/raynier/Development/workspaces/fullstack/vibes/TOWER_WORKSPACE/tower.core/asdlc/product/expert_system_catalog_000118.md).
*   **Target**: Compliance Officers, Internal Audit Teams.
*   **Generation**: Gen 3.

### TOWER-A (Analytic)
*   **Description**: Investor-facing dashboard that turns filed XBRL into high-fidelity financial projections.
*   **Target**: Institutional Investors, Hedge Funds.
*   **Generation**: Gen 3.

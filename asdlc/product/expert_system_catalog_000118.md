## 1. Library: Financial Stewardship & Integrity
*Focus: Protecting the "Rate of Return" and ensuring unassailable forensic accounting.*

### **MOD-FIN-01: ROE Outlier Forensic**
*   **The Mission**: Monitor jurisdictional ROE (Return on Equity) deviations in real-time.
*   **The Skill**: Automatically scrapes multi-state regulatory filings and compares the client's current financials to "Zone of Reasonableness" thresholds.
*   **Value Prop**: Prevents "Sticker Shock" for regulators; provides "Audit-Defense-Ready" data prior to a rate case.

### **MOD-FIN-02: Formula Rate Auditor**
*   **The Mission**: Ensure complex Formula Rate templates are 100% compliant with the latest FERC protocols (Order 864/ARAM).
*   **The Skill**: Maps the **Ladybug Graph** entries directly to the historical recovery formulas, flagging any "Ineligible" costs before they enter a filing.
*   **Value Prop**: Eliminates the risk of "Regulatory Clawbacks."

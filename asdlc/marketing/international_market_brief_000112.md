# International Market Brief: Sustainability & Global ESG (000112)

## Market Opportunity: The Global ESG Mandate
Unlike the US, where regulatory reporting is fragmented, the International market is unifying under the **ISSB (International Sustainability Standards Board)** and the **EU CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive)**. These mandates require high-fidelity **iXBRL** (Inline XBRL) data for sustainability disclosures.

In the international market, TOWER shifts from "Regulatory Compliance" to **"Sustainability Integrity."**

### **Target Persona: The ESG/Sustainability Director**
*   **The Persona**: Responsible for translating operational carbon/waste data into financial-grade disclosures.
*   **The Pain**: "Greenwashing Risk." Ensuring that a narrative claim in a sustainability report is backed by verified data in the ERP.
